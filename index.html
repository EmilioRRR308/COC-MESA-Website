About MESA

At College of the Canyons, the Mathematics, Engineering and Science Achievement (MESA) Program aims to equip financially disadvantaged, underrepresented, and/or first-generation STEM students at COC with the skills and attitudes necessary for their successful transfer to university.

Student Services

Our student study center provides MESA students special services such as:

  • Counseling
  • Tutoring
  • Workshops
  • Computer Use
  • Textbook Checkout
